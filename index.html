About Us

Bread 'n' Butter is a close-knit online gaming community that has one goal - to have fun. We accept players of all skill levels, some players in our community have fun through competition while others enjoy casually playing games. Our only restriction as a community is that you must be charismatic and at least 16 years or older to join!

The most important element in modern-day gaming is a great community , Bread 'n' Butter strives to fulfill that role for all games we play.

Currently Playing: Super Smash Bros. Melee, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, Team Fortress 2, Dota 2
